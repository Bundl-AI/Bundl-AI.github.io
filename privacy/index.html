1. Overview

M9Y AI Pvt Ltd ("M9Y AI," "we," "us," or "our") operates this website and related digital properties for our venture studio and portfolio companies, including Bundl AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you visit our sites, contact us, or interact with our services.

By using our website or providing information to us, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, company name, phone number, and any details you include when reaching out via email or contact forms.
  • Usage data — pages visited, referring URLs, browser type, device type, IP address, and general analytics data collected through standard web technologies.
  • Communications — content of messages you send us, including partnership inquiries, investor outreach, and career applications.
  • Business information — details you provide about your organization when evaluating AI services, consulting engagements, or studio partnerships.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (such as health, biometric, or government ID information) through this website unless you voluntarily provide it and we have a lawful basis to process it.

3. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide information about M9Y AI and our portfolio companies.
  • Evaluate and manage business relationships, partnerships, and investment discussions.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our websites and digital experiences.
  • Send administrative communications, including updates to this policy where required.
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our systems, users, and business.

Where required by applicable law, we rely on appropriate legal bases such as consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, or legal obligation.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Portfolio companies — such as Bundl AI, when you inquire about a specific product or service and we need to route your request appropriately.
  • Service providers — vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, email delivery, and infrastructure, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Professional advisors — lawyers, accountants, or auditors as needed for legitimate business purposes.
  • Authorities — when required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and property.

If M9Y AI undergoes a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to continued protection consistent with this policy.

5. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Retention periods may vary based on the nature of the data and our relationship with you.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information, and to withdraw consent where processing is consent-based. Residents of India may have rights under applicable data protection frameworks as they come into force.

To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@m9y.ai. We may need to verify your identity before responding. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your jurisdiction.

8. Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality.

9. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Material changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
